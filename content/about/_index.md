<p>I'm a networking and infrastructure professional based in the DFW area with a passion for building reliable, scalable systems from the ground up. I currently serve as Assistant IT Manager at Pollo Regio, where I've had the opportunity to design and standardize network architecture across 50+ locations.</p>

<p>From SD-WAN rollouts and Ansible automation to full warehouse buildouts, my path into IT wasn't the traditional one. I spent nearly two decades in retail operations before making the jump, and that experience gave me a strong foundation in problem-solving, leadership, and getting things done under pressure.</p>

<p>Outside of work, I'm always tinkering, whether it's writing scripts to automate something that shouldn't be manual, labbing out new network configs, or exploring whatever technology has my attention that week. This site is where I share what I'm learning along the way.</p>
